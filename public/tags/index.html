Creative Nonfiction

This content is based in real history and personal experience, rather than being wholly fictitious, but it is written with creativity in mind above accuracy.

Critique

Literary critique is a long-standing tradition, and on these pages, I attempt it myself. We critique each other to better understand the literary world ourselves, and to help each other continuously grow.

Tribute

To grow as writers, it is important to acknowledge the broad shoulders of an entire human history upon which we stand. The work on these pages honors those who came before me, and acknowledges their contributions.

Queer

As a queer writer, my reality is inseparable from the queer reality. As such, much of my work reflects queer themes and/or addresses queer issues. Such pages can be find under this tag.

Autobiographical

This tag is applied to any content written by Jester, about Jester.

Interactive fiction is a medium of innovation and endless, branching possibility. From classic Choose Your Own Adventure books to sprawling hubs of hyperlinks, each piece is truly unique.
