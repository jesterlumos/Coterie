About
Last Modified: May 14, 2026

“Here now haue you […] this idle worke of mine: which I feare (like the Spiders webbe) will be thought fitter to be swept away, then worne to any other purpose.” — Sir Philip Sidney.

Traditionally, such intimate and unfinished works as one would find in a literary coterie are shared only with a group of close friends and literary associates. I propose instead that the internet need not be perfectly polished, and that by watching my work change, it can be elevated rather than spoiled.
