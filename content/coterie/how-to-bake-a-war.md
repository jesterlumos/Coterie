the ingredients are simple 
and impossible to find yourself. 
someone else must fetch them for you, 
Sent by curated faith. mix them
gingerly, and note the edges.
those are the terrorists.

it’s up to your eye, Mark the center
from the edge, and heat not an oven, 
but the furnace of Fear. bake 
at 451 fahrenheit until all the terrorist 
Burns off and the center is pure and Alone.

bake another Batch. bake another. 
bake another two, or four, bake More 
just to bake More. start a spreadsheet 
and watch the Numbers reach out and climb. 
sell your first and watch Batches turn to Dollars.

don’t lose sleep over Burnt edges.
don’t smell the Ash, and know 
that what you do not smell is War. eat It. 
eat one War, then three then four at once. 
spend the next few years building
an immunity to War. 

each Batch costs Money,
and you run out of space for endless 
Burnt edges, but the Numbers 
have grown wings, so the only problem 
left is how to never stop Baking.
