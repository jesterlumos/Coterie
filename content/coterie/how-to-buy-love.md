loneliness grows on trees 
like a pit grows in a peach
and only your Wallet can Save you.

plant Chocolate behind teeth
and watch the ground Love sprout
like a Pit grows in a peach.

Love can't come soon enough, 
so Buy it all year round
and watch the ground sprout Love.

Love costs Flowers, must be allowed
to watch beauty Wilt and die,
so Buy them all year round.

Burn Love like the wick of a Candle
poke it until it makes Spectacle,
then watch beauty Wilt and die.

Hunt Love in the air at a Festival,
Tie it down with ribbons and bows,
and poke it until it makes Spectacle,

Buy it away from its homes in poem and prose
because Love is an Expensive Spell,
Tied down with ribbons and bows.

draw st. valentine’s Love from secret marriages
into Spectacular Panopticons of Stuff
because Love is an Expensive Spell.

the Warning grows as a seed in you
like a Pit growing in a peach
that loneliness grows on trees,
and only your Wallet can Save you.
