> whoever for devotion alone, not to gain honor or money, goes to Jerusalem to Liberate the Church of God can substitute this Journey for all penance. 
— Council of Clermont, 1095 C.E. 

the Council has Decreed:

Step 1. abandon the lie of equality. those in power have told you that the other is the equal, can be the equal. they have sold the Purity, the Faith of your America to the global elites and their darkness. 

Step 2. hear the Voice of the Lord: “whom shall I Send? and who will Go for Us?” don’t falter. “Send me!”

Step 3. attend your Commander’s monthly Service, a good Christian Worship, as the Fathers always desired. no ‘gender confusion’ or ‘electric tanks,’ just Singular, Deadly Focus.

Step 4. it will not be a fair Fight, but it is our Sacred Duty. our enemies quiver, and you know, and they know that sooner or later God will Cut them down. "a whole civilization will die, never to be brought back again." this is Peace Through Strength.

Step 5. the dissent is a farce, a distraction. the People love you. the People support you. your friends Respect you, your enemies Fear you, and the whole world feels the Righteousness of the United States Military.

Step 6. don’t be scared. remember, you have immunity in the conduct of your Duties. no savage demon can prevent you from fulfilling your Moral Obligation, your God-Sent Mission. you have earned Indulgence. they will face Justice.
