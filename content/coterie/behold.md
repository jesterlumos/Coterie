> Unlike tattoos and other forms of body art, body painting is temporary 
– Wikipedia

the grown-ups turn your eyes away
from the naked painted street people.

they just look like grown-ups too  
the naked painted street people.

the heat awakens a skin city in you  
with the naked painted street people.  

a neon dragon sails a sea of skin  
of naked painted street people.  

the stories groan and stretch and draw you in  
to the naked painted street people.  

ephemeral-lightning-flash Behold!  
the naked painted street people.

*by Jester Lumos; performed at ASU (2025, [20 poems in 20 days](https://jesterlumos.pages.dev/tags/20-poems-20-days/)*
